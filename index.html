About me

I am an aspiring Instructional Designer with hands-on experience in creating engaging and practical learning experiences for adult learners.

I have worked with Learning Management Systems (LMS), where I managed learners, assigned courses, and tracked performance. This experience has helped me understand how learners interact with digital training and what makes learning effective.

I have recently started creating eLearning courses using Articulate Storyline 360 and Rise 360, focusing on real-world scenarios, interactivity, and learner engagement.

In addition, I run a YouTube channel where I create tutorial-based videos, which has strengthened my ability to simplify complex topics and present information in a clear and structured way.
