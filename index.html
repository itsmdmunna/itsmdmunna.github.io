About me

I am transitioning into Instructional Design from a training background, with 5+ years of experience creating engaging and practical learning experiences for adult learners.

As a trainer, I focused on application-based learning, designing activities and projects that helped learners build real-world skills.

I am now building hands-on eLearning projects using Articulate Storyline 360 and Rise 360, focusing on scenario-based learning, interactivity, and learner engagement.

My experience with Learning Management Systems (LMS) and running a YouTube tutorial channel has strengthened my ability to understand learner behavior and simplify complex topics into clear, structured content.
