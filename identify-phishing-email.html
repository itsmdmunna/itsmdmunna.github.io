Identify A Phishing Email

A scenario-based microlearning course designed for newly onboarded employees to help them recognize and avoid phishing emails.

Experience Project

Problem

New employees are often targeted by phishing emails and may unknowingly share sensitive information without verifying the authenticity of the sender.

Solution

I designed a scenario-based microlearning course to help new employees identify phishing emails through a realistic example. The course focuses on guiding learners to examine email header details and verify the authenticity of the sender through interactive decision-making.

Tools

Articulate Rise 360 and Articulate Storyline 360
