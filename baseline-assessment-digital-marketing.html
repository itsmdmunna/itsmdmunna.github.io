Baseline Assessment - Digital Marketing

An interactive quiz designed to assess learners’ foundational knowledge in digital marketing, featuring personalized results and scoring. 

Experience Assessment

Problem

In a digital marketing training program, baseline assessments were conducted using paper-based methods, requiring manual score calculation. This led to delays in sharing results and made it difficult to provide immediate feedback to learners.

Solution

To address this, I designed a digital baseline assessment using Articulate Storyline 360 that automatically calculates scores and provides instant results upon completion. This eliminates manual effort, reduces delays, and enables learners to receive immediate feedback on their performance.

Tool

Articulate Storyline 360
